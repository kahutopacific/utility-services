Thank you for your enquiry. Kahuto Pacific provides subsurface utility investigation (SUI) across Fiji and the Pacific using Ground Penetrating Radar and EM locating technology.

We've structured our services into three clear levels — choose the one that matches your project scope and budget.

 
Service Levels
LEVEL 1

Site Clearance

 ⚡ Fastest turnaround

Know what's in the ground before you dig. Our crew locates and marks all detectable utilities on your site, and you receive a photo report documenting what was found.

✓Field marks on ground (spray paint & flags)
✓Geotagged site photos
✓SUI Photo Report (PDF)
✓Basic utility schedule
Best for: Contractors, builders & tradies, plumbers & electricians, small works, road openings & reinstatement works and pre-excavation safety
LEVEL 2

Detailed Survey

 ★ Most Popular

Everything in Level 1, plus GPR data post-processed in IQMaps software to give you accurate, calibrated utility depths and a professional report with AS 5488 quality level assignments.

✓Everything in Level 1
✓Processed radargram profiles (JPEG/PNG/TIFF)
✓Excel Utility Report — FMG & WGS84 coordinates, AS 5488 quality level (XLSX)
✓Summary survey report (PDF)
✓Feature pickups exported from IQMaps in two coordinate systems — Fiji Map Grid and WGS84/UTM 60S (DXF)
✓Deliverable Guide — file index, naming convention reference and survey line register (PDF)
➕ Optional Add-On
Combined surface + subsurface utility CAD plans (DXF/DWG/PDF) — request explicitly when enquiring
Best for: Engineers, designers, anyone needing verified depths for design or clash detection
LEVEL 3

Full Design Package

 🏆 Most Comprehensive

Our most complete deliverable. Everything in Level 2, plus a detailed report with recommendations and design-ready CAD/GIS plans that drop straight into your engineering workflow.

✓Everything in Level 2
✓Comprehensive survey report with recommendations (PDF)
✓Combined surface + subsurface utility CAD plans (DXF/DWG/PDF)
✓GIS deliverables (ESRI SHP, KML/KMZ)
✓Full QA/QC checklist (PDF)
Best for: Major infrastructure projects, design-level utility mapping, engineering firms
OPTIONAL ADD-ON

Potholing / NDD Verification

Subcontracted hydrovac excavation to physically expose and verify utility location & depth. Achieves QL-A (highest quality level) per AS 5488. Available with any service level.

◆Physical exposure of utilities at critical crossings
◆Supervised by Kahuto survey crew
◆Backfill & surface reinstatement included
