KAHUTO Utility Locating Services
 

Thank you for your enquiry about our utility locating services.

Kahuto Pacific provides subsurface utility investigation (SUI) services across the Pacific Islands using Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) and Electromagnetic (EM) locating technology, compliant with AS 5488.

We offer three service levels to match your project needs and budget:

LEVEL 1 Fastest turnaround

Site Clearance

Know what's in the ground before you dig. Our crew locates and marks all detectable utilities on your site with spray paint and flags, and you receive a photo report documenting what was found.

✓Field marks on ground (spray paint & flags)
✓Geotagged site photos
✓SUI Photo Report (PDF)
✓Basic utility schedule

Best for: Contractors, small works, pre-excavation safety, "Call Before You Dig" compliance

LEVEL 2 Most popular

Detailed Survey

Everything in Level 1, plus we post-process the GPR data using IQMaps software to give you accurate, calibrated utility depths and a professional survey report with AS 5488 quality level assignments.

✓Everything in Level 1
✓Processed radargram profiles (PDF/PNG)
✓Calibrated utility depths
✓Full AS 5488 utility schedule with quality levels
✓Summary survey report
✓QA/QC validation certificate

Best for: Engineers, designers, anyone needing verified depths for design or clash detection

LEVEL 3 Most comprehensive

Full Design Package

Our most comprehensive deliverable. Everything in Level 2, plus a detailed report with recommendations and design-ready CAD/GIS plans that drop straight into your engineering workflow.

✓Everything in Level 2
✓Comprehensive survey report with recommendations
✓CAD plans (DWG/DXF)
✓GIS deliverables (SHP/GDB)
✓Combined surface + subsurface utility plans
✓Full QA/QC checklist appendix

Best for: Major infrastructure projects, design-level utility mapping, engineering firms

ADD-ON

Potholing / NDD Verification

Subcontracted hydrovac excavation to physically expose and verify utility location & depth. Achieves QL-A (highest quality level) per AS 5488. Available with any service level.

✓Physical exposure of utilities at critical crossings
✓Supervised by Kahuto survey crew
✓Backfill & surface reinstatement included
