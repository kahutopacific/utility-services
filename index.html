Kahuto Pacific provides subsurface utility investigation across Fiji and the Pacific using Ground Penetrating Radar and EM locating technology. Our services are structured into three clear levels, choose the one that matches your project scope and budget.

Service Levels
LEVEL 1
Site Clearance 		Fastest turnaround

Know what's in the ground before you dig. Our crew locates and marks all detectable utilities on your site, and you receive a photo report documenting what was found.

—   Field marks on ground (spray paint & flags)
—   Geotagged site photos
—   SUI Photo Report (PDF)
—   Basic utility schedule

Best for: Contractors, builders & tradies, plumbers & electricians, small works, road openings & reinstatement works, and pre-excavation safety.

LEVEL 2
Site Clearance + GPS Pickups 		 

Everything in Level 1, plus surveyed GPS pickups for every detected utility — delivered as an Excel register in Fiji Map Grid and WGS84 coordinates.

—   Everything in Level 1
—   GPS-located utility pickups (FMG & WGS84)
—   Excel Utility Register (XLSX) — coordinates, utility type, indicative depth
—   Basic AS 5488 quality level assignment

Best for: Clients who need recorded utility positions for site records, GIS upload, or design coordination — without committing to a full processed survey.

LEVEL 3
Full Processing Package 		Most comprehensive

Everything in Level 2, plus full IQMaps post-processing, calibrated depths, AS 5488 quality level assignments, design-ready CAD & GIS deliverables, and a comprehensive survey report.

—   Everything in Level 2
—   Processed radargram profiles (JPEG/PNG/TIFF)
—   IQMaps feature pickups — FMG & WGS84/UTM 60S (DXF)
—   Full AS 5488 quality level assignments
—   Comprehensive survey report with recommendations (PDF)
—   Combined surface + subsurface utility CAD plans (DXF/DWG/PDF)
—   GIS deliverables (ESRI SHP, KML/KMZ)
—   Deliverable Guide & Full QA/QC checklist (PDF)

Best for: Engineers, designers, major infrastructure projects, design-level utility mapping, and engineering firms requiring verified depths for clash detection.

Optional Add-Ons

AVAILABLE WITH ANY SERVICE LEVEL

OPTIONAL ADD-ON

Potholing / NDD Verification

Subcontracted hydrovac excavation to physically expose and verify utility location & depth. Achieves QL-A (highest quality level) per AS 5488.

—   Physical exposure of utilities at critical crossings
—   Supervised by Kahuto survey crew
—   Backfill & surface reinstatement included

OPTIONAL ADD-ON

Drone Photogrammetry + Utility Overlay

We fly the DJI M3E, process an orthophoto, and overlay all utility pickups in CAD. Deliverables: GeoTIFF, JPEG, PDF, ECW + CAD drawing with utility overlay (DWG/PDF). Priced by site area — request when enquiring.
